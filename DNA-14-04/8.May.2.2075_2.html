DNA

 

May 2, 2075

 

I begin to wonder if there may be in the language of biology itself some means of interpreting and "performing" the role of each of my code partners.  For is DNA not a series of words?  And each gene a sentence?  My code partners and I already share a common, nay, virtually identical genetic language.  Is it not a question of merely tapping into these internal resources?  Making the internal explicit?  But I hesitate here, because if psychological mobility is my goal, if I have in fact discovered that he and I are, even based on our biographical differences, the same, the point of my project becomes null and void.
